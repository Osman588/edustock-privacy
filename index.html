EduStock Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

EduStock is a free educational app for Pakistani students. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address and name (when you register)
  • Phone number (if you use phone login)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • App usage data (progress, badges, quiz results)
  • Device information (for crash reporting)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our educational services
  • To save your learning progress
  • To send important notifications
  • To fix crashes and improve stability

4. Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and crash reporting. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Google AdMob

EduStock is free and supported by advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Google Advertising ID (GAID) to serve relevant ads.

  • Opt out: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Google ad policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Admin accounts never see advertisements

6. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen inside the app.

8. Children

EduStock is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Contact Us

Questions? Contact us at: support@edustock.app
